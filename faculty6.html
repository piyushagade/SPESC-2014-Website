    Niraj Topare

    Title Assistant Professor
    Location E 202
    Email niraj.topare@mitpune.edu.in
    Phone +91-8983056527
       




    He has obtained his masters degree in Chemical Engineering from Bharati Vidyapeeth Deemed University, Pune in 2012 and first degree has been awarded by Amravati University in 2010 in Chemical Engineering. His main research area is in Renewable energy sources, Bio-fuels, Bio-diesel production, Waste water treatment, Process modeling & simulation. He is currently involved in the research oriented study on Production of Biodiesel from different feed stocks using heterogeneous and homogeneous catalyst by Ultrasonic process.
