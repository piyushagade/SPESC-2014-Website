    “Our duty is to encourage everyone in his struggle to live up to his own highest idea, and strive at the same time to make the ideal as near as possible to the truth.”
    Swami Vivekananda



    MAEER’s MIT, Pune, India has been constantly engaged in inspiring thousands of students to develop their “Winning Personalities” by making them “Physically strong, mentally alert and spiritually elevated”. With this in mind, at MAEER, we focus on excellent infrastructure and faculty for imparting the highest quality of education, and emphasize on inculcating the right values through discipline and spiritual education. From academics to sports and from spiritual development to personality development, every aspect is important to us, and attention is paid to each of these facets of education.

    The SPE SUB CONTINENTAL MEET 2014 is being organized in the true spirit of World Peace Center which is committed to promote the “Culture of Peace” through a value based ‘Universal Education System’. It is our firm belief that the ‘Union of Science and Religion will alone bring peace to Mankind.’ It fosters a spirit of universal brotherhood by laying emphasis on the pursuit of peace as a way of living and education as a means of attaining the ultimate goal of building up a holistic society.
    I appreciate the active participation of the international student committee and the efforts taken by the teachers and students. I wish that this event will be a medium through which students will be provided with the finest environment for learning, research, innovation and character building
    I thereby welcome students to this inspiring environment and implore them to use this opportunity and their wonderful student years productively.

    Prof. Dr. Vishwanath D. Karad
    Founder, Executive President
    Maharashtra Academy of Engineering &
    Educational Research (MAEER), Pune, India
