  • 2012

    Department of Petroleum Engineering, Maharashtra Institute of Technology, and the Society of Petroleum Engineers, Student Chapter of MIT, organized its Annual Industry Institute Interaction Program–2012 on the 2nd, 3rd, 4th of March 2012.

    The function started with an HR Meet on Friday, 2012. Various representatives from Petroleum and Petrochemical sector participated. The theme of the symposium was “Role of Academic Institutes in Providing Quality Manpower for the Oil and Gas Petrochemical and Chemical Industry ”. The guest lectures were given  by Mr. Raj Khadilkar, Manager, FMC, Mumbai, Mr. Shiva, Manager-Design, WOM, Pune Mr. Yakhob Dwaramakki, Head HR, Transocean, Mr. Avinash Kolte, Recruitment Head, Transocean, Mr. Ravindra Joshi, Rallis India, Mr. Mukul Khare, Mr. R.S. Nageshkar, VP-HP, Mr. Prassanna Shukla, DGM Projects, Mr. Bakshi, Manager, Aker.

    The second part of the symposium, “New and Innovative Technology in Upstream Oil Industry” was organized as a part of the month long series of technical competitions. The prize distribution function and panel discussion were held on March 3rd in the evening for which Mr. Walter Simpson, Managing Director, British Gas India Pvt. Ltd, Mumbai, was the Chief Guest of Honour.

    Student Paper Presentation contest, on innovative ideas was held on Saturday, 3rd March, 2012.

    On Saturday, the function started with the paper presentation competition.

    Dr. P. B. Jadhav, Head, Department of Petroleum Engineering, felicitated the Judges: Dr. Joneik Hage, Discipline Lead, Reservoir Engineer, Shell Technology Center, Bangalore, Mr. Arun Thakur, Technical Professional, Halliburton Offshore Services, Mumbai and P.K. Kamble, Reservoir Engineer, Niko Resources, Baroda.

    During the lunch break, the poster presentation and photography competition was held. This was examined by Mr Prasad, ONGC, Mr. Prakash Kamble and Dr Tanvar, DGH.
