    Siraj Bhatkar

    Title Assistant Professor
    Location E 002
    Email siraj.bhatkar@mitpune.edu.in
    Phone (020) 30273459
       




    His interest lies in the field of Petroleum Production Engineering, Natural gas engineering, Artificial lift, production optimization and Transport of oil and gas. His previous research work was related to Production optimization with hybrid lift technology. Currently his research is in the field of optimizing surface and subsurface choke performance for an oil and gas well.
