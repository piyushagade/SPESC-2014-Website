  • 2006

    Department of Petroleum Engineering, Maharashtra Institute of Technology, and the Society of Petroleum Engineers, Student Chapter of MIT,  organized its Annual Industry Institute Interaction Program – 2006 on the 17th, 18th and 19th March 2006. A symposium, “New and Innovative Technology in Upstream Oil Industry” was organized as a part of this activity.

    Student Paper Presentation contest, on innovative ideas was held on Saturday, 18th March, 2006. Following students presented papers on a wide variety of topics listed below.

    Mr. Iwan Roberts, Sr. LWD Engineer and Mr. Sridhar Amravadi, Account Manager, from Schlumberger Asia Services, Mumbai; Mr. Anil Tyagi (Deputy GM) Reliance Industries (E&P), Navi Mumbai; Mr. P.K. Painuly, GM (Technical), Essar Oil, Mumbai; Dr. Ashish Nagar, Chief Chemist, ONGC, Mumbai; Mr. Shridhar Joshi, Principal Reservoir Engineer, Cairn Energy Pty. Gurgaon and Mr. Luis Salazar, Sr. District Engineer from B.J Services served as judges for the contest.

    A Poster Presentation Competition was held in the Petroleum Geology Laboratory. Esha Verma, Sudeepto Bannerjee, Sameer Hirani, Deepak Patil, Rahul Oswal and Nikhil Jadhav, Vipul Rathod, Neeti Joshi and Madhavi Jadhav presented posters on various aspects of forthcoming technologies in the oil industry.
    A Technical Quiz Competition was organized on the MIT- Lawns on the 17th of March. This included a Movie Clip on Offshore Platforms and Drill Ships of the Oil Industry. Students were asked relevant questions about the movie. Prof. S. J. Naik coordinated this function.

    A series of guest lectures on innovative and new technologies was organized on
    Sunday, 18th of March, covering nearly every aspect of the Petroleum Sector.
