    Pratiksha R. Kale

    Title Assistant Professor
    Location E 103
    Email pratiksha.kale@mitpune.edu.in
    Phone (020) 30273420
       




    Her areas of learning are Particulate Technology, Instrumentation and Instrumentation Analysis. Her previous research work was related to biodiesel synthesis and prediction of fuel properties of biodiesel – diesel blends. Experimental results and model results gives us good accuracy. Currently her research work is on Reactive Crystallization for separation of enantiomers.
