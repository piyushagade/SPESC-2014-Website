    Anand D. Kulkarni

    Title Assistant Professor
    Location E 102
    Email anand.kulkarni@mitpune.edu.in
    Phone (020) 30273420
    Website http://sites.google.com/site/anand123k



    His interest lies in the field of Petroleum Refining, Fuels, Hydrocarbon Thermodynamics and Flow Assurance. His previous research work was related to biodiesel production. Currently his research is in the field of paraffin wax deposition control in crude oil using novel methods. Initial focus is on the method of magnetic fluid conditioning.
