    Shailendra J. Naik

    Title Associate Professor
    Location E 305
    Email shailendra.naik@mitpune.edu.in

    His interest lies in the field of Petroleum Production Engineering, Artificial Lift Techniques, Petroleum Production Operations and Production Optimization. His previous research work was related to Horizontal Well Technology Applications. Currently his research is in the field of Steam Assisted Gravity Drainage Techniques.
