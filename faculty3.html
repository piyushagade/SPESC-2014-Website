    Samarth D. Patwardhan

    Title Associate Professor
    Location E 102
    Email samarth.patwardhan@mitpune.edu.in
    Phone (020) 30273498
       




    He joined the department in the year 2010. Prior to joining academics, he worked for Schlumberger, both in its consulting (DCS) as well as well services (WS) segment. His responsibilities as a production engineer at DCS involved being a part of the Integrated Reservoir Team, which was responsible for providing solutions to clients for their ongoing as well as future projects. His primary responsibility as a Production and Stimulation Engineer (PSE) at WS included delivering stimulation related techno-economic presentations to clients as well as designing, executing and evaluating (DEE) the subsequently pumped job.

    At MIT, he was hired to strengthen the reservoir engineering domain, where he has taught related courses as well as secured research funding from multiple agencies, within three years of joining. Apart from teaching, he is actively involved in industry-academia collaboration efforts. He strives to imbibe a research culture amongst the undergraduate and graduate students at MIT. His research interests include reservoir engineering, reservoir simulation, production enhancement and optimization.
