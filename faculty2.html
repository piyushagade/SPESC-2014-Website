    Rahul Anant Joshi

    Title Assistant Professor
    Location E 305
    Email rahul.joshi@mitpune.edu.in
    Phone (020) 30273420
       



    He did his Post graduation Geology from Department of Geology, University of Pune. Subsequently he started developing interest in Geotechnical Investigation of infrastructure projects. He has worked on investigation projects of jetties, nuclear power plant, tunnels and presently working on SEZ project near Pune. Sir has registered for PhD in Geology from Research Centre in Geology, MIT Pune.
    Apart from interest in engineering geology and rock mass characterization, he is keen in learning sedimentology, seismic interpretation and ichnology.
