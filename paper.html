  • Paper Contest


    MIT Pune SPE student chapter is one of the most active chapters in India. It has been awarded Gold status for the year 2013. As a student organization our mission is to provide each affiliate with opportunities for personal and professional development through the SPE student membership benefits; and also through organization of technical and social activities round the year. Each year, we organize several guest lectures, industrial visit and seminars delivered by distinguished members from academia and from the petroleum industry. We also organize industrial visits and field trips; components that are essential for all-round development of a petroleum engineer .

  • Eligibility


    > Only Student SPE members shall submit and present in the contest.
    > Only full-time bonafide students shall participate in the contest.
    > Students within 9 months of having completed their degree can participate. These students shall submit self declaration and letter from the head of the institute regarding the same.
    > The presentation / paper must not have been either accepted for publication in a peer-reviewed journal at the time of submission for the contest or presented at a previous SPE Regional Student Presentation / Paper Contest.
